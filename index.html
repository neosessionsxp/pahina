Pahina

NEOSESSIONS

"Pahina" or page — and this album is nine of them. NEOSESSIONS' debut full-length moves across love, loss, and the quiet search for self in two languages without ever losing its thread. The carrier single Pahina opens the story; collaborations with Yuki Padilla deepen it. Nine tracks, all written by Kevin Padilla. One unfinished feeling. The session never ends.
